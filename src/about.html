Accessible Duffield is a small charity established as an Unincorporated Association. Our aims are to raise disability awareness, supply staff training, supply advice and access to disability audits & guides.

Our small group are all Duffield residents with interests and experience in disabilities in adults and children. Our wider group includes other volunteers who are able to contribute with achieving our aims of raising awareness and improving village life for everyone.
